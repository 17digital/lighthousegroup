Absolute Trust

In all your ways acknowledge Him, and He shall direct your paths.

Nov. 17th Solid Rock Atlanta

Opening Thought: Show the following picture to everyone in the group. In your own words, describe what is happening in this picture. What do you believe is the message that this picture is conveying?

Opening Scripture

Trust in the LORD with all your heart, And lean not on your own understanding; In all your ways acknowledge Him, And He shall direct your paths.—Proverbs 3:5-6[NKJV]

Discuss

  1. Look again at the image above. Do you believe it is possible to surrender fully to Jesus without learning first to trust Him? Why are trust and surrender interrelated?
  2. Read the Opening Scripture Proverbs 3:5-6. Why must we abandon our own understanding in order to trust God fully?
  3. Read Isaiah 26:3. Why is keeping our mind stayed on Him a form of trust?
  4. Read Psalm 20:7.In this verse, “horses” and “chariots” refer to anything natural in which we place our trust other than the Lord. Are we trusting in something other than JESUS? What are some of the horses and chariots in our life?
  5. JESUS spoke of entering the Kingdom of Heaven as a little child. Do you believe there is a childlike quality to trusting the Lord with all your heart? If so, explain.
