Use his word to prepare your heart.

It's never too early to start, and it's never too late to start. There's no better time to start than now!

Day 49 The Solid Rock Atlanta

Many believers, though they may value the Word of God, have not understood how to use the Word to prepare their heart. For instance, they wait until they are sick before they study Scriptures that speak of healing. In other cases, they wait until they are troubled and full of turmoil in their heart before they meditate on Scriptures that speak of the Divine Peace. Use his word to establish your heart, before you find yourself in a time of need.
